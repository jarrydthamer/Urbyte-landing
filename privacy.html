Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Urbyte Pty Ltd ("Urbyte", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. Who We Are

Urbyte Pty Ltd is an Australian technology company building a platform that converts unused mobile data into DBUs — a digital currency you can spend, cash out, or donate to Australian charities.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at: privacy@urbyte.com.au

2. What Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us voluntarily, including:

  • Your name and email address (via our waitlist sign-up form)
  • Your mobile carrier and plan details (to calculate unused data)
  • Any message or feedback you submit to us

We may also collect technical information automatically, including your IP address, browser type, and pages visited, for analytics and security purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Add you to our waitlist and send you product updates
  • Improve our platform and user experience
  • Respond to your enquiries
  • Comply with our legal obligations

We will not use your information for any purpose that you would not reasonably expect.

4. Marketing Communications

By joining our waitlist, you consent to receiving product updates and launch information from Urbyte. You can opt out at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email we send, or by contacting us directly at privacy@urbyte.com.au.

5. Disclosure of Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Trusted service providers who assist us in operating our platform (e.g. email delivery, analytics), under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Law enforcement or regulatory bodies where required by law

6. Overseas Disclosure

Some of our service providers may be located overseas (e.g. the United States). Where we disclose information to overseas recipients, we take reasonable steps to ensure those recipients handle your information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from misuse, loss, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. Our platform uses industry-standard encryption and security practices.

8. Access and Correction

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you and request corrections if it is inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated. To make a request, contact us at privacy@urbyte.com.au. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Complaints

If you believe we have breached the Australian Privacy Principles, you may lodge a complaint with us at privacy@urbyte.com.au. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

For any privacy-related questions, email us at privacy@urbyte.com.au
