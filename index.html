Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Urbyte and DBUs.

DBU stands for Data Backed Unit. It's Urbyte's digital currency — 1GB of unused data = 10 DBUs, each valued at $0.05 USD. Think of them as loyalty points, but backed by something real: your unused mobile data.

You submit your monthly telco bill to Urbyte. We calculate how much data went unused that cycle and convert it into DBUs at a rate of 10 DBUs per GB. After 3 billing cycles of verification, your balance unlocks for redemption.

Spend them on rewards and gift cards, convert them to cash back, or donate to Australian charities like Beyond Blue, Orange Sky Australia, 1800RESPECT, and more. You choose.

The 3-cycle window protects you. It lets us verify your actual average unused data before crediting your wallet — so your DBU balance is accurate and fair. Founding Members get their bonus DBUs immediately; the wait applies to data-backed conversions.

We're building support for all major Australian carriers — Telstra, Optus, Vodafone, TPG, Boost, and more. Join the waitlist and tell us your telco so we can prioritise accordingly.

Think of a bigger prepaid recharge as an investment — the excess pays you back in DBUs.
