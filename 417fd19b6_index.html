How Urbyte Works

Simple, transparent, yours.

📱
STEP 01

Log Your Usage

Enter your monthly mobile plan and how much data you actually used. Takes 30 seconds.

⚡
STEP 02

Earn DBUs

Urbyte converts your unused data into DBUs — Data Backed Units. 1GB = 10 DBUs. Your data, your value.

❤️
STEP 03

Choose What Matters

Spend DBUs on rewards and cash back, or donate directly to Australian charities that need it most.

❤️

Your data can change a life

Donate your DBUs directly to Australian charities making a real difference. No middlemen. No admin fees. Just your unused data, doing good.
